About Me

I have been paid as a software developer for the last twenty years. Now you noticed that I did not write that 'I worked' and the reason is I enjoyed every minute of it .. and still do today!

I have said this to many young people who are deciding on a career, and that is choose something that you love to do and you will never 'work' for living

Now I am not saying it was all easy - in fact at times it was very difficult - but I love to be challenged to figure out a solution to problem.

I never regreted chossing this profession. I grew up in the Bronx NY never dreaming that I travel the world - and I did and it was because I did something that I love.
